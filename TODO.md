# Planned Features & Ideas

## Dynamic Amperage (Pending CEE Socket Installation)
*Currently postponed until the CEE socket is installed (currently using Schuko).*

**Idea:** Automatically adjust the charging current based on the electricity price.
- **Negative / Extremely Cheap Prices:** Maximize the charging current (e.g., 16A / 11 kW) to pull as much energy as possible during the short, highly profitable time window.
- **Normal Cheap Prices:** Reduce the current (e.g., 8A or 10A) for a more battery-friendly and grid-friendly charge over a longer period.
